Using :nth-child() in CSS

:first-child 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:last-child 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-child(even) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-child(odd) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-child(2) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-child(4n) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-child(n+3) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-child(-n+3) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-child(3n+1) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-child(3n-1) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
:nth-last-child(3) 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
